Features

Everything your team needs

Built by accountants, for accountants. Every single feature solves a real problem experienced in live operations.

Payment Workflow
The complete digital payment lifecycle — from first request to final confirmation.
🔄
Customisable Approval Chain
Set your own approval steps — 2, 3 or 4 levels. Assign any role to any step. Visual Workflow Builder. Changes apply instantly.
✍️
Digital Signatures
Upload your signature image once. Auto-stamped at the right step with your name, designation, and date. No printing needed.
🔢
Auto PV Numbering
Unique PV numbers auto-generated: PS/BML/MVR/26/001. Entity code, bank, currency, year, sequence. Resets per entity per year.
🚨
Urgent Payment Flags
Mark a payment urgent. All approvers instantly notified. URGENT badge on PV throughout entire workflow.
🏢
Multi-Entity Support
Manage multiple group companies from one dashboard. Each entity has its own PV numbering, bank accounts, and reports.
🏦
Staff Bank Account Selection
Staff register their personal bank accounts. When submitting, they select where to receive funds — no guessing for the accounts team.
📊
Real-Time Status Dashboard
Every user sees their PV status live: Submitted, Checking, Pending MD, Approved, Paid. Filter by entity and export.
💱
Multi-Currency
MVR and USD active by default. Admin enables any currency — AED, INR, LKR, EUR, GBP and more. Each tracked separately.
❌
Reject with Full History
MD can reject at any stage. Rejection, reason, and timestamp logged permanently. Submitter notified immediately.
Documents & Filing
Every document tracked, every version preserved, every package auto-built. Audit-ready from day one.
📦
Merged PDF Package
Once paid, PayFlow merges the signed PV + all supporting docs + payment slip into one PDF. Auto-named: PV-Number_Supplier_Amount.pdf
🖨️
Print PV (Customisable)
Print a professional PV to A4. Admin controls which fields print, custom text boxes, accent colour, font, and branding via the PV Template editor.
🖨️
Cheque Printing
Print-ready 210×89mm layout for BML, MIB, MCB, BOC, CBM. Amount in words auto-generated. Print directly on cheque leaf.
↺
Replace Attachments
Wrong invoice attached? Replace with one click. Old file kept permanently for audit — visible to accounts team, invisible to staff.
👁
Role-Based Document Access
Staff see only their own uploads and the payment slip. Accounts team see everything including replaced versions. Built-in by design.
🕐
Full Audit History
Every action — submission, forwarding, check, approval, document replacement, payment, printing — recorded in a visual timeline with name, role, date, time.
Finance & Accounting
Built on IFRS principles, for real finance teams managing multiple entities.
📒
Chart of Accounts
Full IFRS-aligned COA with 38+ accounts. Admin can add accounts anytime. Standard numbering across Assets, Liabilities, Revenue, Expenses.
🔑
Ledger Permissions per Role
Toggle matrix for each role. Staff see only expense accounts. Dept heads see operational accounts. Accounts team sees everything. Prevents miscoding.
💳
Payment Confirmation
MD sees full beneficiary details — bank, account, SWIFT — alongside company paying account. Bank reference, date, and slip upload all in one screen.
📈
Reports & Analytics
Pending checking, approval, payment, paid PVs, entity breakdown, urgent report — all filterable by entity, date, currency. Export to Excel.
💱
Currency Management
Admin enables currencies from a built-in list. Only active currencies appear on the Submit form. Add custom currencies not in the default list.
🏦
Company Bank Accounts
Manage all company bank accounts per entity. BML, MIB, MCB, BOC and more. Accounts team selects the correct paying account per PV.
Admin & Control
Full control over how PayFlow works for your company — no IT knowledge required.
🔧
Form Builder
Admin controls every field on the Submit PV form. Toggle visible/hidden, mark required, rename labels. Add custom fields — Project Code, Cost Centre, and more.
🔄
Workflow Builder
Visual approval chain builder. Add, remove, reorder steps. Assign any role to any step. Changes apply to all new PVs immediately.
🔑
Ledger Permission Matrix
Toggle-matrix for each role. Select all, clear all, or individual accounts. Changes take effect immediately for all users in that role.
👥
User & Password Management
Create users, assign roles, set passwords with strength enforcement, force change on first login, reset any user, remove leavers.
📸
Profile Photos
Every user uploads a profile photo. Shows in topbar, user lists, and the audit trail — makes the team feel like real colleagues.
🖨️
PV Print Template
Customise every aspect of the printed PV: fields shown, custom text boxes, accent colour, font, page size, footer. Live preview before saving.
Integrations
Connect PayFlow Pro to the tools your finance team already uses.

🟣
Odoo ERP
REST API connection. Paid PVs sync as vendor bills or payment journal entries in Odoo's accounting module.
🤖
Claude AI Assistant
Built-in help chat powered by Claude (Anthropic). Available 24/7 inside the app. Answers any PayFlow question instantly.
📧
Email Notifications
Real-time email alerts at every step. Submission, urgent flags, approval, payment confirmation with slip attached.
🏦
Maldivian Banks
Cheque printing for BML, MIB, MCB, BOC, CBM at 210×89mm. Beneficiary bank details managed per supplier.
📄
PDF Engine
Server-side PDF generation merges PV + attachments + payment slip into one document. Auto-named, PAID-stamped, auto-archived.

FAQ
Common questions
How does the free trial work?▾
Sign up and get 30 days free on any plan. You can save a card (no charge for 30 days, billed automatically after) or skip the card and pay by bank transfer at the end of your trial.
Can I customise the approval workflow?▾
Yes — on Business and Enterprise plans you can add, remove and reorder steps and assign any role to any step via the visual Workflow Builder. Changes apply instantly to new PVs.
Does it work outside Maldives?▾
Absolutely. PayFlow Pro is a global platform. Cheque printing is optimised for Maldivian banks, but all other features work for any country. Admin can enable any currency.
What is the AI Help Assistant?▾
A built-in help chat powered by Claude (Anthropic). Ask any question about PayFlow features — submitting PVs, workflows, ledger accounts, printing — and get instant answers 24/7.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?▾
Yes. Upgrade anytime from the Subscription page inside the app. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle. Email billing@yenuit.com to change plan.
Is my data secure?▾
Yes. Each company's data is fully isolated (multi-tenant row-level security). All data encrypted at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed — never stored in plain text.
